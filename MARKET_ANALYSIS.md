## 4. Key Strategic Insights for Our Platform

### 4.1 Universal Gaps (None of the 3 competitors have):

1. **AI-powered property search** with NLP queries
2. **Interactive 3D tours** or WebGL property viewers
3. **Working WhatsApp integration** with click-to-chat
4. **EMI/mortgage calculators** on property pages
5. **Interactive floor plans** (SVG-based unit selection)
6. **Progressive lead capture** (multi-step, reduced abandonment)
7. **Client portal/dashboard** for post-inquiry tracking
8. **Blockchain/tokenization** readiness
9. **IoT/sustainability dashboards** with real-time data
10. **Schema.org structured data** for real estate SEO

### 4.2 Best Practices to Adopt:

- **From Balaji**: Dark luxury theme, project categorization, gated brochure downloads
- **From Akus**: Detailed project specs, floating contact widget, service diversification, heritage branding
- **From None (we innovate)**: 3D tours, AI search, progressive forms, WhatsApp, animations

### 4.3 Architecture Decisions:

- **React + Vite** (per project config) with TypeScript
- **Tailwind CSS** for utility-first responsive design
- **shadcn/ui** for accessible, customizable primitives
- **Framer Motion** for premium animations
- **WhatsApp Business API** integration
- **react-hook-form + Zod** for validated lead capture
- **Mobile-first** design (70%+ searches are mobile)
